My Working Experiences

Cyber Intel Internship at PwC

In 2024's summer, I completed a Cyber Threat Intelligence Analyst shadow experience at PwC’s Hong Kong office, supporting clients in strengthening their security posture against emerging threats. Specifically, I authored three cyber threat intelligence reports profiling high-risk threat actors and their tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs), which informed targeted mitigation recommendations and strengthened detection and response playbooks for multiple enterprise clients. I also shadowed associates during cybersecurity tests, observing how simulated attacker behavior is used to uncover vulnerabilities and validate new security controls. I helped validate new client security controls and contributing external threat landscape insights from open-source and industry feeds to support more accurate risk assessment and prioritization.
